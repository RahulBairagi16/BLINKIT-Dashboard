# BlinkIT Sales Dashboard | Power BI

This repository contains an interactive **Power BI Dashboard** analyzing sales performance, outlet trends, and customer behavior for **BlinkIT** (formerly Grofers).  
The goal of this project is to derive **data-driven insights** to help optimize product sales, outlet performance, and overall business growth.

---

## Project Overview

The **BlinkIT Sales Dashboard** provides a detailed visualization of key metrics including total sales, average rating, item visibility, and outlet performance across multiple regions and categories.  
The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by presenting patterns, trends, and correlations between products and store attributes.

---

## Key Objectives

- Analyze **total sales** across various outlet types and locations.  
- Identify the impact of **item visibility** and **establishment type** on sales.  
- Examine **average rating trends** across product categories.  
- Compare **performance across outlet sizes and tiers**.  
- Provide actionable insights for improving sales and operations.

---

## Dashboard Features

- **KPI Cards:** Display total sales, average rating, and number of items sold.  
- **Interactive Filters:** Filter by outlet type, item type, and location.  
- **Sales Distribution Charts:** Visualize sales by item type and outlet establishment.  
- **Outlet Performance Analysis:** Compare performance of different store types and sizes.  
- **Customer Insights:** Understand buying patterns and product preferences.

## Insights Gained

- Tier-3 outlets generated **lower sales** but **higher average ratings**, indicating potential growth opportunities.  
- **Supermarket outlets** consistently outperform grocery stores in total sales.  
- **Item visibility** directly correlates with higher sales, emphasizing the need for product placement optimization.  
- Certain **item types** (like snack foods and soft drinks) drive a significant portion of total revenue.
